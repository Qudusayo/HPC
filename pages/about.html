ABOUT HPC

The HPC Vision

Increased professionalism, profitability, and freedom for those in the trades.

The HPC Mission

To help those in the trades have a greater impact in their businesses, in their lives, in their families, and in their communities.

Why High Performing Contractor?

Because struggling sucks! I know, I was there. I spent the long days and nights working - missing time with my family and friends - just to get by. It was not fun... It was not fulfilling.

Through years of this, I was able to break free, and find the habits and characteristics that produce success. I was able to gain the freedom and fulfillment in my business, the industry, and with my family. I was able to make a greater impact on all those around me. I was able to become a High Performing Contractor!

At that point, I decided I wanted to help others in the industry to develop their habits and characteristics, gain their freedom, and have their success and fulfillment. To build a business that provides them the lifestyle they desire, so they too could be High Performing Contractors.

What makes a High Performing Contractor? Studies have shown that those that perform at the highest levels obsess over six particular things more than average people. They do so when it comes to their businesses, their lives, their families, and their communities. High Performing Contractors are no different! These six things are...

- CLARITY -

High Performing Contractors are very clear on their vision, direction, purpose, and mission. They have clarity of who they are, who they need to be, and who they need to surround themselves with. They also know what they are doing, why they are doing it, and how they are going to make it all work. They are intentional!

- ENERGY -

High Performing Contractors know how to generate the energy they need to accomplish their goals and fulfill their vision. They have the energy to provide better products, handle the tasks of running their company, and be the leader their team needs; while still being able to actively engage when they get home!

- REASON -

High Performing Contractors know what they are fighting for! They know that it is truly deeper than money and wealth. They have understanding of why they are showing up - every day -and outperform those around them. This gives them the urgency to be great, and drives them to be High Performing.

- PRODUCTIVITY -

High Performing Contractors get things done! They know how master their strengths, delegate their weaknesses, and accomplish far more than their competition. They don't let pride or distraction get in their way of goals, and learn new skills they need to further their company.

- INFLUENCE -

High Performing Contractors know how to positively influence those around them. They influence from a place of kindness, honesty, and humility. They are able to build a community around them, a culture within that community, and connect with their employees, clients, vendors, subcontractors, and family.

- COURAGE -

High Performing Contractors lean into the fight. They step up and take risk. They know that nothing good gets started without courage; and courage helps them gain competence, and produce outstanding confidence - more than other contractors! They run towards the fire, and welcome the risk.
